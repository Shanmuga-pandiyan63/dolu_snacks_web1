Our Options

we believe that life’s greatest moments are made even better with the perfect bite. Whether you’re looking for a quick treat, a savory snack, or something to satisfy your sweet tooth, our diverse menu has something to delight every palate.

Savory Snacks

Gourmet Fries: Perfectly crispy fries with your choice of gourmet toppings, from truffle oil to parmesan cheese. Mini Sliders: Juicy mini burgers served with a variety of toppings, from classic cheddar to spicy jalapeño. Nacho Platter: Loaded nachos with melted cheese, guacamole, sour cream, and your choice of toppings.

Sweet Treats

Churros: Golden, crispy churros dusted with cinnamon sugar and served with a rich chocolate dipping sauce. Ice Cream Sandwiches: Creamy ice cream sandwiched between freshly baked cookies – a perfect blend of warm and cold. Waffle Bites: Bite-sized waffles topped with fresh fruit, whipped cream, and a drizzle of maple syrup.

Healthy Options

Fruit Parfaits: Layers of fresh fruit, creamy yogurt, and crunchy granola for a refreshing and healthy treat. Veggie Cups: Crisp, fresh vegetables served with your choice of dipping sauce – perfect for a light and guilt-free snack. Smoothie Bowls: Nutrient-packed smoothie bowls topped with fresh fruit, nuts, and seeds for a delicious and energizing snack.

About

we believe that snacking is more than just a way to fill a hunger gap – Our mission is to offer a variety of mouth-watering snacks that cater to every craving, whether you're in the mood for something savory, sweet, or healthy.
